About

ROSHANI-NGO is a voluntary organization registered under society Registration ACT 1973 Regd.No. 13053/2003, dated 30/12/03 in the states of Madhya Pradesh. The Executive Committee members are directly or indirectly involved with the development of our society. Organisation working for the promotion of downtrodden in its operational area covering rural, urban, tribal, villages of Betul Distt. (M.P.), Having noticed that the people of these villages are very backward in respect of Education, Health, sanitation environment, women empowerment, agriculture utilizing Government programs etc. Roshani initiated poor and downtrodden communities in the society by promoting its welfare activities besides Government welfare programmes, Roshani looks forward to work for the promotion of Women by creating awareness among the poor and needy women of rural & urban areas to various income generation and skill development.
